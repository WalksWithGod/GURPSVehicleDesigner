B5 Tech Level

GURPS has a well-worked out Tech Level system and this system puts Earth’s later half of the 20th century (1950-2000) in and around Tech Level 7. In contrast the Star Trek universe of the 23rd and 24th centuries seems to be around TL13-15.

Based on what is seen on the B5 and what is in the BP books, The Earth Alliance is in early TL10, with some variations in artificial intelligence (around TL9), FTL communications (TL11), and medical technology (around TL8).

Sense the Narn seem less technologically advanced than the humans I have placed them at TL9 with different levels of technology they have gotten from other races. This would make the Minbari at a high TL13 or early TL14 and would place the Centauri at high TL 11 to a low TL12. The Vorlons and the Shadows each have access to unknown levels of high technology (TL16+?). It may be wise to make role-playing encounters with the Vorlons and the Shadows limited. The PCs may however encounter the servants of the Shadows sense they are closer to the PC’s races tech level.

The other alien races that are independent and or those among the League of Non-Aligned Worlds could have a basic tech level of high TL8 to low TL9. Many of them would also have purchased of TL9, TL10, or higher items such as FTL Jump Gates. Of course there are a great many races out there that no one has heard of. Maybe your PCs will run into a race of beings that are at TL5 in all things except space travel. Their space travel technology is at TL9 and they bought that from some unscrupulous trader. After all in an infinite universe all things are possible.
